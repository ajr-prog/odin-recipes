Steak Tartare

Create an amazing dish with simple steps! Steak tartare is a classic gourmet dish, once said to have been invented by the tartare tribes when they had no time to cook. Eat it like pate!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound finely ground beef tenderloin
  • 1 teaspoon brown mustard
  • ½ teaspoon hot pepper sauce (e.g. Tabasco™), or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon brandy
  • 1 pinch salt, or to taste
  • ground white pepper to taste
  • 1 egg

Steps:

  1. In a medium bowl, mix together the beef, mustard, hot pepper sauce, Worcestershire sauce, brandy, salt, pepper and egg until well blended.
  2. Arrange the meat in a neat pile on a glass dish, and cover with aluminum foil.
  3. Refrigerate for 30 minutes to allow the flavors to blend. Serve as a spread on crackers or toast.
